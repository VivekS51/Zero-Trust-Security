# Threat Scenarios

---

## Scenario 1

### Guest Wi-Fi Lateral Movement

Attack

A compromised passenger phone attempts to reach the POS network.

Expected Defense

- Network Segmentation
- Zero Trust Policy
- Firewall Rules
- Continuous Verification

---

## Scenario 2

### Stolen Crew Credentials

Attack

An attacker steals an engineer's credentials and attempts login from an unknown device.

Expected Defense

- MFA
- Conditional Access
- Device Compliance Check

---

## Scenario 3

### Insider Threat

Attack

A POS employee attempts to access HR records.

Expected Defense

- RBAC
- Least Privilege
- OPA Authorization

---

## Scenario 4

### Satellite MITM

Attack

Satellite communication experiences interception and packet manipulation.

Expected Defense

- TLS Encryption
- Certificate Validation
- Local Cached Policies

---

## Scenario 5

### GDPR Violation

Attack

Sensitive passenger information is exported outside EU jurisdiction.

Expected Defense

- Compliance Policy
- Data Classification
- Export Restrictions
